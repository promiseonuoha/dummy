What is the minimum amount required to start trading?

The minimum amount to start trading depends on the plan you choose, but typically, you can begin with as little as 500 USD for the Basic Plan.

How do I deposit funds into my account?

You can deposit funds through bitcoin transfer. Simply go to the 'Deposit' in your Dashboard and transfer the bitcoin worth the trading plan you choose.

Is my money safe on the platform?

Yes, we prioritize security and use encryption and secure payment gateways to protect your funds and personal information.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

To withdraw, go to the 'Withdraw' section of your account, select your preferred withdrawal method, and follow the instructions. Withdrawals typically take 6 hours to process.

What assets can I trade on the platform?

We offer a wide range of assets, including stocks, cryptocurrencies, commodities, forex, and indices. You can explore the full list on our platform.

What trading tools do you provide?

We offer advanced charting tools, technical analysis indicators, real-time market data, and educational resources to help you make informed trading decisions.

Can I trade on mobile devices?

Yes, our platform is fully optimized for mobile devices, allowing you to trade anytime, anywhere through our mobile app or responsive website.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page, and follow the instructions to reset it.

How do I get in touch with customer support?

You can reach our customer support team 24/7 via email, or phone. Visit the 'Contact Us' page for more details.

Is the company registered?

Yes, it's registered with the FCA.
